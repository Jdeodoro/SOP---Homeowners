Standard Operating Procedure - SOP

Inserting a Homeowners Policy in AMS360

Overview

This is a step-by-step on how to add a Homeowners policy to AMS360 system. For training purposes, not all information were filled in.

Step 1: Navigate to Client Account

Action: Open AMS360 and locate the client's account for whom you are adding the homeowners policy.

Details: Click on 'Costumer' and use the search bar find the correct client. Click on the hyperlink on the client's name to open their account details.

Step 2: Add New Policy

Action: Within the client's account, navigate to the "Policies" tab. Click on the "Add Policy" or "New Policy" button.

Details: This button is typically located in the toolbar or a dedicated section for policy management. A new window or form will appear.

Step 3: Create New Policy (Personal Lines)

Action: Fill out the necessary fields with the policy informations, like policy number, carrier and insurer. Then click ok to continue.

Details: This selection will load the appropriate fields and forms for a homeowners policy.

Important: Selecting the wrong line of business will require starting over.

Step 4: Basic Policy Information

Action: Fill in all required policy details, specially the total premium amount.

Details: Refer to the policy declaration page for accurate information. Ensure all fields marked as mandatory are completed.

Step 5: First Named Insured

Action: Fill in all fields with the name of the policy owner(s), In this case, Marcus & Katherine Di Matthew, and mailing address.

Details: For training purposes, not all fields need to be filled in.

Step 6: Line of Business

Action: Select the line of business, check if the fields are correct, add a description if needed then press add.

Details: After adding the line of business, click on the hyperlink to open another tab to insert coverages and rating information.

Step 7: Adding Premissses, Coverages and Rating information

Action: In the new tab, press add to include the premisses of the covered residence. Fill out the premisses address, coverages, rating information and additional interest, if there's any. Upon completing all required fields, press save and close.

Details: For training purposes, we're not filling all of the available fields.

Step 8: Adding an Activity and Suspense

Action: After adding all the important and required information of the policy to the system, press save and close and you will be redirected to all the costumers policies. Select the one you just inserted, press new activity on the right side of the screen to add an activity, attachments and a follow-up date.

Details: A new tab is going to open to be filled in with descriptions, dates and attachements.

Step 9: Activity and Suspense

Action: Fill all the important information, classify the policy as new business, add a description to be followed-up and select a date for your account manager to review and approve the policy to send.

Details: It's very important to not forget to attach the policy PDF.

Step 9: The end.

Action: After adding the activity, attachment and follow-up, you save and close and will be redirected to the costumer's account. You'll notice that when you select the policy you just added, a new activity tab will be available to check under activities, on the right side of the screen.

Details: In 7 days AMS360 will remind the account manager to follow-up the directions added to the activity.

Congratulations!

You have successfully entered a homeowners policy into AMS360.
